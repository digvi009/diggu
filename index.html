Preserving Literature. Inspiring Generations.

Ramesh Jijaram Nevase, born on June 5, 1947, in Pune, is a distinguished writer and journalist with a deep passion for Maharashtra’s rich historical heritage. Over the years, he has explored more than 30 forts across the state, gaining extensive knowledge and insight into their history, architecture, and cultural significance. Remarkably, at the age of 70, he authored 18 books in 2017 alone—each dedicated to a different fort—offering readers a detailed and engaging account of Maharashtra’s glorious past. His lifelong dedication continues to inspire history enthusiasts and fort lovers across the country.
